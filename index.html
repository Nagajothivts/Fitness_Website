FIT-OASIS FAQ

Your Questions Answered

How do I register?
To register, please visit us in person, complete the registration form, and embark on your
fitness journey immedietly.
Do you offer free trials?
Absolutely , we provide a complimentary one-day trial for individuals who are new to
Fit-Oasis.Each person will recieve designated timing.
Do you offer refunds?
we regret to inform you that we do not offer refunds.Please note that this policy is clearly
outlined in your registration form as well.
What is the registration fee?
We do not have a registration fee at Fit-Oasis .Our prices include all charges, and no
additional fees are required. Additionally , free water refills are included.
Can memberships be put on hold?
membership can only be placed on hold for medical reasons up to 1 week under valid
documentation. Special membership can be hold upto 2 weeks.
Do you have a ladies' hour?
At Fit-Oasis , we do not have a designated ladies' hour as we are a unisex gym, catering to
all members equally.
